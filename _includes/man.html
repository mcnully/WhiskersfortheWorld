The Man
Pastor Austin Gardner

Austin Gardner is currently serving as the pastor of Vision Baptist Church in Alpharetta, Georgia. Before starting Vision on March 12, 2006 his family served God for 20 years in Arequipa, Peru as church planting missionaries. Betty and he married on August 18, 1973 at Fellowship Baptist Church in Rome, Georgia. God has blessed them with a great family.

Austin Gardner shaves every morning.

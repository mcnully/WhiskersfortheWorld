"Movember" or November for Whisker Laity

That most virile of seasons is once again upon us. It is time for Movember. As a way to celebrate such an occasion Vision Baptist Church has fused two things we love a great deal. World Evangelism with the Gospel and facial hair. Whiskers for the world begins 1 November at the stroke of midnight and ends promptly 30 November at 11:59:59 pm ET.

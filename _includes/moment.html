No-Shave November

Did you hear what I said? He shaves EVERY morning

As a way to celebrate No-Shave November @ Vision Baptist Church this year we have fused two things we love a great deal: World Evangelism and facial hair. Whiskers for the world begins 1 November at the stroke of midnight and ends promptly 30 November at 11:59:59 pm ET.

Whiskers for the world is a pledge drive for one man (see above) to perform one task (not shave for 30 days) in exchange for a daily pledge amount by you. Every day he does not shave you are committing funds towards World Evangelism.
